The Club

What your daughter joins.

🎓

A real path to college

98 total college commitments, 55 of them girls — with 75 of those coming since 2021 alone.

🏆

Proven on the national stage

Our U16, U17 and U18 Boys and Girls National teams have qualified for the USAV Championships numerous seasons since 2021. Multiple age groups qualify year after year.

🏐

Coaching that's actually played

95% of our coaches have played professionally, internationally, or collegiately — across 25 girls teams from U11 to U18.

📍

Built around your schedule

All practices in Porter County, twice weekly in season, with small-group lessons available. Regional teams stay within 1.5 hours — no required hotel stays.

🚗

Travel that makes sense

National & College Prep tournaments stay within a 3–4 hour radius: Chicago, Indianapolis, Louisville and Toledo. Choose the level of commitment that fits your family.
